About the Song

"Aroma" is a riveting Punjabi number which balances profound emotion with vivid and blistering images. Here, the artist talks about trials, karma, and a close relationship between his soul and the world. The chorus presents an antithesis to the artist's dark past and the sweet fragrance, indicating purity and positivity of his soul. The verses take us further into Sidhu's journey, beginning with a hint of that past filled with struggles and destiny but then unfurling layers with metaphors like the perfume of "heer'an"(diamonds) and the stench of bad "karma." Sidhu's style of storytelling makes his music come alive with a motif recognizable to his fans, expressing his struggles, growth, and finally peace with his path.

About the Artist

Shubhdeep Singh Sidhu is popularly known as Sidhu Moose Wala. He was an excellent singer, songwriter, and rapper who popular in the whole world for his words and of bold music. His style of mixing Punjabi folk to hip-hop and modern sounds was really great much of his songs speak about identity, struggle, and social issues. His rough and unapologetic style got a huge fan-following not only within India but outside also. Some of his songs, such as Just Listen,So High and Aroma turned out to be the anthem of youth culture. People in the year 2000 worked in a different way for just a little while and ended his life in 2022, but his music will always remain to be there to inspire and reach millions of fans worldwide, thus continuing to be among the finest examples of modern Punjabi music.
